# market-facing positioning (draft)

## what adoption-eval is
`adoption-eval` is a practical method for turning workflow pain into decision-ready AI pilots.

## the problem
Most organisations do not struggle to find AI tools. They struggle to choose the right intervention, define safe boundaries, and prove results.

## what is different here
- starts with workflow opportunity discovery, not model selection
- compares multiple intervention paths (including non-AI)
- treats boundaries as design requirements, not afterthoughts
- evaluates outcomes beyond speed: time, cost, quality, risk, adoption, control confidence

## who it is for
- AI adoption leads
- ops and transformation teams
- consultants
- technical teams translating business requirements into implementation paths

## value proposition
From discovery to pilot decision in one coherent method:
- discover
- qualify
- match
- bound
- pilot
- evaluate
- decide

## what buyers get
- clearer prioritisation of opportunities
- safer intervention choices
- faster, smaller pilots
- transparent go/no-go decisions

## what this is not
- not a generic AI maturity survey
- not model hype
- not automation-first regardless of context
